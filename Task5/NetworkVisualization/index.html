Help
  • Double click to create a new node.
  • To rename a node, click on its label.
  • To change a node's role, click while holding down the Shift key.
  • To delete a node, click it while holding down the Alt key.
  • To connect two nodes, click them while holding down the Ctrl key.
  • To disconnect two nodes, click on their connecting edge while holding down the Alt key.
  • To strengthen an edge, click on it while holding down the Shift key.
  • To weaken an edge, click on it while holding down the Ctrl key.
